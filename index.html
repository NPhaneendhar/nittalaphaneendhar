My Work

PHANIX Investigation Expert

Launch Tool

Phanix is a comprehensive web-based Digital Forensics & Cyber Investigation platform. It empowers investigators with a suite of browser-based tools to analyze digital evidence, verify integrity, and uncover hidden data without installing complex software.

  • Integrity Scanner
    Calculates cryptographic hashes (MD5, SHA-256) to verify that digital evidence remains unaltered and authentic.
  • Comparison Tool
    Performs bit-by-bit comparison between files to detect unauthorized modifications or data corruption.
  • Metadata Analyzer
    Extracts hidden EXIF and also data from images to reveal device details, timestamps, and geolocation.
  • Steganography
    Analyzes images to detect hidden messages or payloads using LSB (Least Significant Bit) analysis.
  • Password Audit
    Evaluates password strength entropy and simulates dictionary attacks to identify weak credentials.
  • Network Logs
    Parses and visualizes network traffic logs to identify suspicious IP connections and potential anomalies.
